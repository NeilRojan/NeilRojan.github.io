About Me

My name is Neil Rojan. I am currently pursuing my BTech in Information Technology at Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology. Born and brought up in Mumbai, I moved to native Kerala for my graduation after completing my schooling until the 12th grade. Passionate about blending technology with management, I aim to leverage my skills in data analytics and machine learning to build a meaningful career.


Professional Journey

As an aspiring graduate student, my focus has always been on acquiring knowledge and honing my skills to contribute significantly to society. My role as a Millennium Fellow for the class of 2021 led me to spearhead a mental health drive aligned with the United Nations SDG 3. My leadership experiences, including serving as the IEEE Student Branch Secretary and MUN Club Secretary, underline my commitment to teamwork and organizational excellence. A highlight of my leadership was playing a pivotal role in hosting a MUN conference after a 2-year hiatus due to COVID. Currently, I am involved in the Signer project, focusing on ASL to English translation, and the Stat Sport App, an entrepreneurial venture aimed at revolutionizing sports engagement.
